John Havlicek was born in Martins Ferry, Ohio.

When was John Havlicek born?

John Havlicek was born on April 8, 1940.

How tall was John Havlicek?

John Havlicek was 6-5 (196 cm) tall.

How much did John Havlicek weigh when playing?

John Havlicek weighed 203 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

Is John Havlicek in the Hall of Fame?

John Havlicek was inducted to the Hall of Fame as a Player in 1984 (Full List) .

When was John Havlicek drafted?

John Havlicek was drafted by Boston Celtics, 1st round (9th pick, 9th overall), 1962 NBA Draft.

What position did John Havlicek play?

Small Forward and Shooting Guard.

When did John Havlicek retire?

John Havlicek last played in 1978.

What did John Havlicek average?

John Havlicek averaged 20.8 points, 6.3 rebounds, and 4.8 assists per game.

How many rings does John Havlicek have?

John Havlicek won 8 championships.

What schools did John Havlicek attend?

John Havlicek attended Bridgeport in Bridgeport, Ohio and Ohio State.

What are John Havlicek's nicknames?

Hondo, Country Boy, Captain John are nicknames for John Havlicek.
