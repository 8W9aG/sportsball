How old is Larry Bird?

Larry Bird is 68 years old.

Where was Larry Bird born?

Larry Bird was born in West Baden, Indiana.

When was Larry Bird born?

Larry Bird was born on December 7, 1956.

How tall is Larry Bird?

Larry Bird is 6-9 (206 cm) tall.

How much did Larry Bird weigh when playing?

Larry Bird weighed 220 lbs (99 kg) when playing.

Is Larry Bird in the Hall of Fame?

Larry Bird was inducted to the Hall of Fame as a Player in 1998 (Full List) .

When was Larry Bird drafted?

Larry Bird was drafted by Boston Celtics, 1st round (6th pick, 6th overall), 1978 NBA Draft.

What position did Larry Bird play?

Small Forward and Power Forward.

When did Larry Bird retire?

Larry Bird last played in 1992.

What is Larry Bird's net worth?

Larry Bird made at least $25,870,000 playing professional basketball.

Larry Bird made $7,070,000 in 1992.

What did Larry Bird average?

Larry Bird averaged 24.3 points, 10.0 rebounds, and 6.3 assists per game.

Larry Bird won 3 championships.

Larry Bird attended Springs Valley in French Lick, Indiana and Indiana State University.
