Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Ben Roethlisberger?

Ben Roethlisberger is 43 years old.

When was Ben Roethlisberger born?

Ben Roethlisberger was born on March 2, 1982.

Where was Ben Roethlisberger born?

Ben Roethlisberger was born in Lima, OH.

How tall is Ben Roethlisberger?

Ben Roethlisberger is 6-5 (196 cm) tall.

How much did Ben Roethlisberger weigh when playing?

Ben Roethlisberger weighed 240 lbs (108 kg) when playing.

Is Ben Roethlisberger in the Hall of Fame?

Ben Roethlisberger has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

How many games did Ben Roethlisberger play?

Ben Roethlisberger played 249 games over his career.

How many passing yards did Ben Roethlisberger have?

Ben Roethlisberger had 64,088 passing yards over his career.

How many touchdowns did Ben Roethlisberger have?

Ben Roethlisberger had 418 touchdowns over his career.

Ben Roethlisberger has won 2 Super Bowls.

When did Ben Roethlisberger retire?

Ben Roethlisberger last played in 2021.
