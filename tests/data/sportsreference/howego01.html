Frequently Asked Questions

When was Gordie Howe born?

Gordie Howe was born on March 31, 1928.

Where was Gordie Howe born?

Gordie Howe was born in Floral, Canada.

How tall was Gordie Howe?

Gordie Howe was 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did Gordie Howe weigh when playing?

Gordie Howe weighed 205 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

Is Gordie Howe in the Hockey Hall of Fame?

Gordie Howe was elected into the Hockey Hall of Fame as a Player in 1972.

How many goals did Gordie Howe have?

Gordie Howe had 801 goals over his career.

How many points did Gordie Howe have?

Gordie Howe had 1,850 points over his career.

How many Stanley Cups has Gordie Howe won?

When did Gordie Howe retire?
