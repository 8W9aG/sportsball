Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Todd Gurley?

Todd Gurley is 30 years old.

When was Todd Gurley born?

Todd Gurley was born on August 3, 1994.

Where was Todd Gurley born?

Todd Gurley was born in Baltimore, MD.

How tall is Todd Gurley?

Todd Gurley is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much did Todd Gurley weigh when playing?

Todd Gurley weighed 224 lbs (101 kg) when playing.

Is Todd Gurley in the Hall of Fame?

Todd Gurley has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

How many games did Todd Gurley play?

Todd Gurley played 88 games over his career.

How many rushing attempts did Todd Gurley have?

Todd Gurley had 1,460 rushing attempts over his career.

How many rushing yards did Todd Gurley have?

Todd Gurley had 6,082 rushing yards over his career.

How many Super Bowls has Todd Gurley won?

Todd Gurley has never won a Super Bowl.

When did Todd Gurley retire?

Todd Gurley last played in 2020.

What is Todd Gurley's Instagram account?

Todd Gurley is on Instagram at tg4hunnid.
